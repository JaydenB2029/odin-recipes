Red Velvet Cake

Description

Red Velvet Cake is a classic dessert known for its striking red color and rich flavor. This recipe offers a moist cake with a hint of cocoa, layered with cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk, room temp
  • 2 large eggs, room temp
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • For cream cheese frosting:
  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vinegar, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients into wet, mixing just until combined.
  5. Divide batter evenly into pans. Bake 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool cakes in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  • Make frosting: Beat cream cheese and butter until smooth, add vanilla, then gradually beat in powdered sugar until fluffy.
  • Frost cooled cakes and stack them.
