Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert made with cream cheese, sugar, and eggs on a graham cracker crust. This recipe is simple and delicious, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • For the crust: 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • For the filling:
  • 32 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream, room temp
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs, room temp

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Mix crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Bake 10 minutes, then set aside.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until very smooth (no lumps). Mix in sour cream and vanilla. Add eggs one at a time, mixing just until combined (don’t overbeat).
  3. Pour filling over crust. Place pan on a baking sheet. Bake 55–65 minutes, until edges are set but center is slightly jiggly.
  4. Turn off oven, crack the oven door, and let cheesecake sit inside for 1 hour (prevents cracking). Then cool at room temp before refrigerating at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Top with fruit, whipped cream, or leave it plain and creamy!
