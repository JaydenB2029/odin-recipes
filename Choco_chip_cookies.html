Chocolate Chip Cookies (This recipe makes ~24 cookies)

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic American dessert, known for their gooey chocolate centers and crispy edges. This recipe is easy to follow and results in delicious cookies every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips (or chunks)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then mix in vanilla.
  4. In another bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually stir into wet ingredients.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop ~2 tbsp of dough per cookie onto baking sheet, leaving space between each.
  7. Bake 9–11 minutes, until edges are golden but centers are still soft.
  8. Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire racks.
